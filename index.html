About Endgrain Hardwood Co. — Handcrafted in Atlanta, Georgia

Every piece is handcrafted right here in Atlanta, Georgia from premium hardwoods and creative materials. No shortcuts. No particle board in the hardwood pieces. Just thoughtful craftsmanship designed to last.

All wood is either repurposed or sustainably sourced from downed trees in the Atlanta, GA area — so every purchase supports local, sustainable woodworking. When a tree comes down in metro Atlanta, it doesn't have to become mulch. It becomes something beautiful.

The record trays each feature a real upcycled vinyl album cover as the base, and the wall art pieces add bold hand-painted color to solid wood and craft wood surfaces. Functional art for your home, made the right way.

Made with love & care in my Atlanta shop. Shipped to your door.
