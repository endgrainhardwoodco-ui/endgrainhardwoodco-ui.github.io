About Endgrain Hardwood Co.

Every piece is handcrafted from premium hardwoods — walnut, maple, oak, and cherry. No shortcuts. All wood either repurposed or sustainably sourced from downed trees in the Atlanta, GA area. No particle board. Just solid wood built to last.

The record trays? Each one features a real upcycled album cover, giving new life to vintage vinyl. Functional art for your home.

Made with love & care in my shop. Shipped to your door.
